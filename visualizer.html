Lab Trend Visualizer

Track one lab test at a time so you can see whether your results are increasing, decreasing, or staying stable over time.

How to use this tool:
1. Enter the name of the lab test you want to track.
2. Enter your lab results separated by commas.
3. Put the results in oldest to newest order.
4. Add your lab’s reference range if you have it.
5. Click Track My Lab Pattern to see the trend and chart.
