# 💬 Scripts for the Exam Room

Use these scripts when you feel your data is being dismissed or your symptoms are being minimized.

### Scenario: The Doctor ignores your AI Data
**Doctor:** "I don't really trust smartwatches or apps."
**You:** "I understand these aren't diagnostic tools on their own. However, I have tracked a **statistically significant 30-day trend** that correlates 100% with my symptoms. How can we use this objective data to refine my treatment plan?"

### Scenario: The "Hard Stop" (Refusal to Test)
**You:** "If you are refusing to run this test despite the trend shown in my biometric data, I request that you **document your specific clinical reasoning for this refusal in my chart** before I leave today."

### Scenario: Addressing Brain Fog
**You:** "Because I am experiencing cognitive impairment (brain fog) during this flare, I am using an AI scribe to record our summary so I can follow your instructions accurately."
