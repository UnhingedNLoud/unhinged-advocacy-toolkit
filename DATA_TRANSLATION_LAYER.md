# 🔍 The Data Translation Layer

Doctors often ignore "feelings," but they listen to "clinical observations." Use this table to translate what your AI apps are telling you into language that gets results in the exam room.

| If the AI App says... | You tell the Doctor... |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Your sleep was 40% restless." | "I am experiencing significant nocturnal symptom interference." |
| "Heart Rate Variability (HRV) is low." | "My biometrics indicate a high inflammatory load and poor recovery." |
| "Your 'Battery' or 'Energy' is 10%." | "I am at baseline exhaustion with objective signs of post-exertional malaise." |
| "Flare predicted in 24 hours." | "I have identified a prodromal phase of my flare cycle via biometric trends." |
| "Skin temperature is elevated." | "I am tracking a trend of localized hyperthermia correlating with pain." |

---
**Advocacy Tip:** Bring the raw data (the app screen), but lead the conversation with the "Medical Language."
